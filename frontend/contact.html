Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a book to the library?

To add a book, you need to create an author account and login. Then navigate to the "Add Book" page and fill out the book information form. You can manage all your added books in the "My Books" section.

Can I purchase books through PaperTrail?

Currently, PaperTrail is a digital library management platform. You can browse books, add them to your cart and wishlist. The checkout functionality is available for demonstration purposes.

How do I become an author?

When signing up, select "Author" as your account type. This will give you access to add and manage your own books in the library. You can edit or delete your books anytime from the "My Books" section.

Is there a mobile app?

PaperTrail is currently a web-based platform that works on all devices through your browser. The website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing.

How do I report inappropriate content?

If you encounter inappropriate content, please contact us through this form with the details. Our team will review and take appropriate action within 24-48 hours.

Can I edit or delete my books?

Yes, as an author, you can edit or delete books you've added through the "My Books" section on the Add Book page. Simply click the Edit or Delete button on any of your books.

How does the wishlist work?

You can add books to your wishlist by clicking the heart icon on any book. Your wishlist helps you save books for later, and we'll show you recommended books based on your preferences.

Can I change the theme?

Yes! You can toggle between light and dark mode using the theme toggle button in the navigation bar. Your preference will be saved for future visits.
